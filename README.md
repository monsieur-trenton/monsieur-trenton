# John Trenton

**French educator and builder of language learning tools.**

I teach AP French at Pinnacle High School in Phoenix and spend the rest of my time building [Concordance Learn](https://concordancelearn.com), a proficiency-focused platform for French learners and their teachers. The platform is designed around ACTFL/CEFR alignment, Bayesian knowledge tracking, and AI-assisted practice -- built in public, one feature at a time.

---

## What I'm building

### [Concordance Learn](https://concordancelearn.com) &nbsp; `active`
A French language learning platform targeting students and teachers from novice through AP and IB levels. Features include proficiency-aware AI conversation practice, adaptive vocabulary exercises, teacher dashboards with early-warning flags, and AP essay preparation. Spanish is next.

### [text-generator](https://github.com/monsieur-trenton/text-generator) &nbsp; `open source`
A standalone tool for generating contextualized vocabulary gap-fill activities. Built with Vite. Free to use.
